Ingredients:
- One cup of water
- A handful of freshly cut ginger
- One tablespoon honey
- Some juiced lime


Directions:
- Peel and slice the ginger.. the thinner the better (I.e. maximize the surface).
- Boil the ginger in the water for at least 10 minutes (The longer the better).
- Add the lime and honey (both optional)


Great for soothing stomach aches, aiding digestion, boosting the immune system or
just for tasting a nice tea.
